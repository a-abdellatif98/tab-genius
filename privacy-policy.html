Tab Genius Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

Tab Genius is a browser extension that helps you organize and manage your tabs. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Access

To provide tab categorization and management features, the extension accesses:

  • Open tab titles and URLs — Used only to categorize tabs (e.g., Social Media, Work, Documentation) and suggest actions. This data is processed entirely on your device.
  • Your settings — Such as bookmark location, default category actions, and custom categories. Stored locally in your browser (Chrome storage).
  • Saved session names — If you use the session save feature, session names are stored locally.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not send any of your data to external servers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information.

Data Processing

All categorization and analysis runs locally in your browser. No tab data, URLs, or titles leave your device.

Chrome Sync

If you have Chrome sync enabled, your settings may be synced across your signed-in Chrome browsers. This is controlled by Chrome, not by Tab Genius.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address provided in the Chrome Web Store listing.
